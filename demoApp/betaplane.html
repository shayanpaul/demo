STORIES WORTH SHARING

June 22, 2012

Sound proof
ideas in flight feature

We are always looking for ways to improve upon our service at Delta. One way we do this is through Ideas in Flight, which is located within Facebook page. Here, you can submit our next great idea to make our service even better. Jonathan Dixon submitted an idea through the page saying, "I'd really like a white noise channel so I can tune out of what is going on around me and tune in to my work."

We liked Jonathan's idea and thought that it seemed like it would work well aboard the Beta Plane. After all, most of our passengers are traveling for business purposes and of those, almost all say that they havebrought work on a plane at lease once before. So we took Jonathan's bright idea and decided to test five white noise channels to find out which one keeps the plane the quietest and which you like best.

Kudos to Jonathan for making the Beta Plane a better place.
